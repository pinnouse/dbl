Pokécord

Add to your server

Pokécord is a bot bringing Pokémon to Discord. It allows users to catch pokémon that appear randomly in their servers, train them, battle with their friends or trade them to complete their collection. All you have to do is add the bot to your server!

Catch

Pokémon will randomly appear in your server, compete with your friends to be the fastest to catch it and add it to your collection!

Battle

Level up your pokémon then battle your friends to gain more XP and credits to be used in the shop!

Collect

There's no limit to how many pokémon you can catch, so catch 'em all!
